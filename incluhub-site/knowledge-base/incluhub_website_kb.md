## Audience and Role Structure
### Creators Ecosystem Hierarchy
1. Owners (highest authority)
2. Artists (mid-level collaboration layer)
3. Talents (entry-level growth layer)

### Owners
- Identity: Full ecosystem control.
- Access: Full access.
- Capabilities: platform-wide analytics, oversight, collaboration control, network governance.

### Artists
- Identity: Build and collaborate.
- Access: Partial access.
- Capabilities: hire/collaborate with talents, execute projects, access creator database.

### Talents
- Identity: Apply and grow.
- Access: Limited access.
- Capabilities: apply for opportunities, grow profile visibility, participate in projects.

## Platform Principles
- Dedicated manager support for guided actions.
- Zero-commission narrative for creators (as shown in website philosophy sections).
- Free access narrative for brands to discover and connect with talent.
- Premium UX with smooth transitions and clean information hierarchy.
